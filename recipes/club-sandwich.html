Description

A hearty, multi-layered sandwich made with toasted bread, sliced turkey, ham, crispy bacon, lettuce, tomato, and mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 2 slices of bread, toasted
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 leaves of lettuce
  • 2 slices of turkey
  • 2 slices of ham
  • 2 slices of tomato

Steps

  1. Place bacon in a heavy skillet.
  2. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown and drain on paper towels.
  3. Spread each slice of bread with mayonnaise.
  4. On one slice of toast, place the turkey, ham, lettuce, bacon and tomato. Top with last slice of toast.
