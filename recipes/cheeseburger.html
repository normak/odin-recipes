Description

A delicious sandwich that features a juicy beef patty topped with melted cheese. Served in a soft, toasted bun along with classic toppings like lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles, and condiments such as ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 slice of cheddar cheese
  • 1 set of hamburger buns
  • 1 leave of lettuce
  • 2 slices of tomatoes
  • 2 onion slices
  • 2 pickle slices
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of mustard
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Form patty and press a small dent in the middle.
  2. Season patty with salt and pepper.
  3. Pan-fry patty over medium heat for 4-5 minutes per side. Add cheddar cheese in the last minute and let it melt.
  4. Toast hamburger buns until golden brown.
  5. Assemble: Place lettuce, tomato, pickles, and cheesy patty on the bun. Add condiments.
